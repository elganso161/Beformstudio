About us

Irina Kiseleva - architect, co-founder of the studio, organizer of most of the processes in the team.

Anna Pavlova - architect, one of the founders of the studio

Igor Rudyak is a 3D visualizer directly related to the work of the team.

The Team

Our story began while studying at St. Repin. Our team members were trained at the Faculty of Architecture of the St. Petersburg Academy of Arts. After graduating from the university almost simultaneously (in 2010 and 2011) and receiving diplomas in one specialty - "Artist-architect".

We decided to continue our common activity in the design of interiors and furniture in the retail sector, working in conjunction with the production of furniture for shops. This is how the idea of ​​creating a design studio appeared in 2014. For such a short but productive period of time, we have gained invaluable experience in understanding the features of materials, construction and installation of commercial equipment.

The Studio

An important role in the development of our studio was played by the experience of working with large foreign chains in the mass market. We were able to successfully adapt European design concepts at Russian sites. We are very proud of the continuity of European experience in the development of our brand.

If you are interested in creating a competent, unique and memorable commercial space, we will be glad to see you among our clients!
